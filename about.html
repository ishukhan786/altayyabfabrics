About Al-Tayyab Fabrics Hangu

A Legacy of Quality in Hangu

Best Fabrics Shop in Hangu

Al-Tayyab Fabrics has been at the forefront of providing premium textiles and fashion in Hangu for years. Located near Shahi Masjid, we are the destination for those seeking the finest fabrics and luxury accessories.

We pride ourselves on our curated selection of men's, women's, and children's wear, alongside an exclusive collection of perfumes and timepieces. Every product in our store is chosen with an eye for detail, quality, and timeless style.
